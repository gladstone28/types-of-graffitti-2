Exploring Paste-Up Street Art

Discover the vibrant and expressive world of paste-up art, a dynamic form of street expression that combines the immediacy of graffiti with the craftsmanship of printmaking.

This allows you to delve into the world of paste-up art, a form of street art where images or texts are printed on paper and then pasted onto a surface in a public space, often with wheatpaste. This method allows artists to prepare their work beforehand and quickly deploy it in various locations.:
