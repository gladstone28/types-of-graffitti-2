Welcome to Our Digital Art Gallery

Explore a curated collection of urban art from around the world, featuring everything from vibrant murals and intricate stencils to impactful street art and guerrilla installations. Each piece tells a unique story, offering insights into the culture, politics, and social issues of its environment. Dive into the world of urban creativity and be inspired by the power of public expression.

Mural

Mural - Large paintings or artworks done on walls, usually in public spaces, that are more formalized and often commissioned or sanctioned.

Tag

Tag - A stylized signature of a graffiti artist, often done in spray paint or markers.

Stencil

Stencil - Art created by using a template to apply paint or ink to surfaces, commonly used in graffiti to produce clear, repeatable designs.

Street Art

Street Art - Visual art created in public locations, typically unofficial and outside the context of traditional art venues, encompassing various forms including graffiti.

Vandalism

Vandalism - Acts of defacing or damaging property without permission, which can include graffiti but has a negative connotation, emphasizing the illicit aspect.

Paste-up

Paste-Up - A form of street art that involves creating images or text on paper and then pasting them onto surfaces in public spaces.

Mosaic

Mosaic - Art made by assembling small pieces of colored glass, stone, or other materials, which can sometimes be found in public or outdoor settings, similar to murals.

Public Art

Public Art - Art in any media that has been planned and executed with the intention of being staged in the physical public domain, often accessible to all.

Installation Art

Installation Art - Three-dimensional works designed to transform the perception of a space, which can be temporary or permanent and often found in public spaces.

Guerilla Art

Guerrilla Art - Artwork that is installed, performed, or applied in public spaces without official authorization, similar to graffiti in its rebellious spirit and often overlapping with street art.
